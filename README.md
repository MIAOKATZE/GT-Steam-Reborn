A GregTech New Horizons expansion mod that **supplements the Steam Age and significantly expands steam usage**, providing 19 multiblock steam machines, 5 single-block nodes, 13 types of hatches, and a Hub-Node binding system. It fills the gameplay gap between the steam age and the electric age in GTNH, making steam a viable and deep progression path rather than a transient phase.

一个 GregTech New Horizons 扩展模组，**补充蒸汽时代并显著拓展蒸汽用途**，提供19台多方块蒸汽机器、5个单方块节点、13种仓室以及枢纽-节点绑定系统。它填补了 GTNH 蒸汽阶段到电力阶段之间的玩法空白，让蒸汽成为一条可行且有深度的进阶路线，而非过渡阶段。

## Highlights / 亮点

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTESteamHubArray.png" width="400"></p>

**Hub-Node Binding System / 枢纽-节点绑定系统** — Cross-chunk and cross-dimensional fluid transfer and remote operations. 3 hubs (Steam / Water / Singularity Drilling) + 6 node types.  
枢纽-节点绑定系统——跨区块、跨维度的流体传输与远程作业。3 大枢纽（蒸汽/蓄水/奇点钻井）+ 6 种节点。

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTESingularityDrillingHub.png" width="400"></p>

**Singularity Drilling Hub / 奇点钻井枢纽** — A marvel of the steam age. Drives mining and drilling nodes to extract resources across dimensions. Requires superheated steam.  
奇点钻井枢纽——蒸汽时代的奇迹造物。驱动采矿与钻井节点跨维度攫取资源。需要过热蒸汽。

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTELargeSteamFurnace.png" width="300"> <img src="README/MTEAirCompressor.png" width="300"></p>

**Steam Processing / 蒸汽加工** — 9 machines inheriting `MTESteamMultiBlockBase`: furnace, compressor, centrifuge, fluid drill, crust borer, singularity compressor, and more. Support superheated steam 4× speed.  
蒸汽加工——9 台继承 `MTESteamMultiBlockBase` 的机器：熔炉、压缩机、离心机、流体钻井、地壳掘进机、奇点压缩机等。支持过热蒸汽 4 倍速。

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTELargeCokeOven.png" width="300"> <img src="README/MTESiemensMartinFurnace.png" width="300"></p>

**Enhanced Processing / 强化加工** — 10 machines inheriting `MTEEnhancedMultiBlockBase`: coke oven, Siemens-Martin furnace, geothermal boiler, steam turbine array, solar overpressure array, kinetic processing array, gear steam compressor, ammonia plant, and hub arrays.  
强化加工——10 台继承 `MTEEnhancedMultiBlockBase` 的机器：焦炉、平炉、地热锅炉、蒸汽轮机机组、太阳能超压阵列、动力加工阵列、机械蒸汽压缩机、制氨工厂及枢纽阵列。

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTEMegaSteamTurbineArray.png" width="400"></p>

**Mega Steam Turbine Array / 巨型蒸汽轮机机组** — 12-tier power generation from steam. Stacking efficiency scales with layers.  
巨型蒸汽轮机机组——12 级蒸汽发电，堆叠效率随层数增长。

<p align="center"><img src="README/MTEKineticProcessingArray.png" width="400"></p>

**Kinetic Processing Array / 动力加工阵列** — Superheated-steam-driven dynamic recipes. Insert any single-block machine to process its recipes with parallel and speed bonuses.  
动力加工阵列——过热蒸汽驱动的动态配方。放入任意单方块机器即可获得并行与速度加成。
